Who we are

KOLL Ltd is a local logistics company that has the capabilities to provide solutions for all of your delivery needs. KOLL currently owns and operates a fleet of cargo vans and concentrates exclusively its efforts in developing a dense network for rapid and reliable servicing in urban environment for its corporative partners and their delivery needs.

Our business foundation is based on customer satisfaction. We are dedicated to getting the job done and we aim to offer the best solution based on our deep knowledge about the industry. KOLL offers personalized and innovative customer service to other corporate entities and institutions in a rapidly growing depersonalized industry.

KOLL is a company that grows at a sustained and solid pace of increase. Our growth is based on our dedication to customer satisfaction by providing reliable, responsive, competitive, and efficient service. It is our consistent service and exceptional communication with our clients that sets us apart from other companies.

Services

KOLL’s flexible cooperation solutions and optimal pricing help our partners to maintain the margins they need to thrive. Moreover, our exceptional dedication and determination help our customers to achieve their goals and customer commitments thus allowing them to compete at the highest level.
